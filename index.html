How to Find the Best Flight Deals: Checklist

Not sure where to go? Use the "Explore" option in Google Flights to find destinations that match your interests and budget.

Search for the lowest priced round-trip (RT) and one-way ticket prices for the next six months. This gives you a baseline price.

Find the lowest priced flight to your destination, then find a different returning flight path. This can save money and eliminate backtracking.

Search for destinations near or on the way to your desired location. Two one-way flights or flights to a nearby destination might be cheaper.

Check flights from various airports, including small regional ones. The additional travel time might be worth the savings.

Create a spreadsheet using Google Sheets or Excel to compare options. Include date, from, to, price, and ticket type.

Add flights to a watch or alert list to stay updated on price changes.

Keys to Success:

  • • Be Flexible: With dates, times, and destinations.
  • • Embrace Red-Eye Flights: Save time and money by traveling overnight.
  • • Think Creatively: The cheapest flight path may take you to unexpected places.
  • • Use Incognito Mode: Search in private or incognito mode to prevent price changes.
  • • Check Small or Local Airlines: Look for local flight operators and search their websites directly.
  • • Understand Airline Pricing: Timing matters. Finding the right moment to buy can lead to significant savings.
